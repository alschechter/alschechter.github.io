## I work with both cosmological simulations and observational data to study the role galaxy mergers play throughout cosmic time.

### I investigated star formation and black hole accretion in galaxy mergers in the IllustrisTNG50 simulation.
I compared the specific star formation rates and specific black hole accretion rates of galaxy mergers to a mass matched sample of nonmergers from 0.2 < *z* < 5. I found that galaxy mergers have more sSFR and sBHAR, and that this excess often increases with galaxy mass and decreasing redshift.

### I train convolutional neural networks to better identify galaxy mergers in imaging.
I created mock images from the IllustrisTNG simulation to use as my initial training set. I am currently working on using interpretable AI techniques to learn what features of mid to high redshift mergers are before applying domain adaptation techniques to identify mergers in *HST* and *JWST* imaging.
